Basics of Web Design HTML5 & CSS3

Overview

Basics of Web Design HTML5 & CSS3 provides a foundational roadmap for aspiring designers, transitioning from core Internet principles to the practical application of structuring and styling websites using HTML5 and CSS3. The book covers essential industry standards such as accessibility, search engine optimization, and responsive design to ensure high usability across various platforms. Concluding the logistics of domain acquisition and site publishing, it serves as a holistic toolkit for developing professional, well-optimized digital environments.
