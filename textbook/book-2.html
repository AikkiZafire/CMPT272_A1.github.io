E-Commerce 2013: Business, Technology, Society

Overview

E-Commerce 2013: Business, Technology, Society provides a comprehensive framework for understanding the field through three major driving forces: business development, technology change, and social controversy. This 9th edition explores the full breadth of the digital marketplace, from the technical infrastructure of the Internet and mobile platforms to the evolving strategies of online marketing and retailing. Laudon and Traver also address critical ethical issues, including privacy, intellectual property, and government surveillance, preparing students to navigate the complex legal environment of modern e-business.
