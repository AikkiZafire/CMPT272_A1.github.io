Global Marketing Management

Overview

Global Marketing Management stands as the authoritative guide to the strategic opportunities and challenges of the international marketplace. In this Eighth Edition, Warren J. Keegan moves beyond simple export strategies to explore how firms can leverage global scale while adapting to local culture. The text offers in-depth analysis of the "Standardization vs. Adaptation" debate and provides updated case studies on entering emerging economies like the BRICS nations (Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa). It is designed for students who aspire to lead in a borderless economy, focusing on the managerial competence required to design and execute global marketing programs.
