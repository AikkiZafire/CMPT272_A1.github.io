Database Processing

Overview

Database Processing offers a solid foundation in the fundamentals of database design, modeling, and implementation. In this Twelfth Edition, Kroenke and Auer bridge the gap between traditional theory and modern practice by introducing vital Cloud Computing concepts and "Big Data" technologies. The text provides extensive hands-on coverage of SQL, covering enterprise standards like Oracle Database, Microsoft SQL Server, and MySQL. It is widely praised for its clear explanation of normalization and entity-relationship data modeling, making complex architectural concepts accessible to beginning developers.
